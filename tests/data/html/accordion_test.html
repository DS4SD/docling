Account Information FAQ

1. How can I get a digitally signed bank statement?

You can download your statement from the online banking portal. Navigate to "Accounts" → "Statement" in the menu, select your account and time period, then click "Download" and choose the digital signature format.

Account Information FAQ
2. How do I update my contact information?

You can update your contact details through the online banking portal. Go to "Settings" → "Personal Information" and edit your contact details as needed.
